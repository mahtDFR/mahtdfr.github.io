Ultimaker

Since 2017 I’ve worked at Ultimaker, a leading desktop 3D printing company with offices in the Netherlands, USA, and Singapore. Ultimaker’s mission is to accelerate the world’s transition to local manufacturing and digital distribution. During my time here I’ve worked within the Marketing and R&D departments, and I’ve produced various forms of content to attract customers to the Ultimaker brand.

Landing pages

Ultimaker Cura
Ultimaker Cura is a popular slicing software for 3D printing. This landing page is the most visited page on Ultimaker.com, with millions of hits per month.

Ultimaker materials
Ultimaker’s compatible material range is highlighted with this landing page. A filter-by-property mechanism helps customers find the materials they need quickly, and is extremely useful for material showcasing at industry trade shows.

Ultimaker applications
Demonstrating a range of proven applications that Ultimaker 3D printers can be used for helps prospective customers understand why they might need one (or more!)

Localization

In 2019 I worked on a project to translate Ultimaker.com’s buying journey into German, French, Italian, Spanish, Korean, and Japanese. Thanks to this, Ultimaker is now able to reach thousands more global customers in their local language.

White papers

I’ve researched and written several white papers to persuade business decision makers to adopt 3D printing technology:

5 ways to stop wasting money on production processes
Examines how large brands have embedded Ultimaker 3D printers in their processes to bring products to market faster, while slashing prototyping costs.

Important 3D printing software features
Examines the important role of software in the 3D printing workflow, and how to get started quickly.

DFRobot
