Here are some projects I’ve been involved in:

Ultimaker

Since 2017 I’ve worked at Ultimaker, a leading desktop 3D printing company with offices in the Netherlands, USA, and Singapore. Ultimaker’s mission is to accelerate the world’s transition to local manufacturing and digital distribution. During my time here I’ve worked within the Marketing and R&D departments and have produced various forms of content to attract customers to the Ultimaker brand.

Landing pages

Ultimaker Cura
Landing page for Ultimaker Cura, a popular slicing software for 3D printing. This is the most visited page on Ultimaker.com with millions of hits per day.

Ultimaker materials
Highlighting Ultimaker’s compatible material range. A filter-by-property mechanism has been extremely useful for industry trade shows.

Ultimaker applications
Showcasing proven industrial applications for Ultimaker 3D printers.

Localization

In 2019 I worked on a project to translate Ultimaker.com’s buying journey into German, French, Italian, Spanish, Korean, and Japanese. Thanks to this, Ultimaker has been able to reach thousands more global customers in their local language.

White papers

I’ve researched and written several white papers to persuade business decision makers to adopt 3D printing technology.

5 ways to stop wasting money on production processes
Examines how large brands have embedded Ultimaker 3D printers in their processes to bring products to market faster and at reduced cost.

Important 3D printing software features
Examines the important role of software in the 3D printing workflow, and how to get started quickly.
